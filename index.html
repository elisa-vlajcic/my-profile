~ My international background ~

The first thing I like to say to people when I meet them, is that despite being Belgian, I actually never lived there. Not even a day. Weird, right ?

Both of my parents come from Belgium , which is why I was born there. However, at the time, they were living in Germany . I spent 8 years of my life there (in Bonn and a small village near Frankfurt), before moving to France (Lille, to be more precise). I graduated from an international High school before moving to the Netherlands for my Bachelor degree. I studied International Business Administration in Rotterdam, where I lived for 3 years. I was also lucky enough to go on exchange to Singapore for 5 months during my last Bachelor year. Since August, I'm back in France and living in the small (but cute) village of Jouy-en-Josas, (not so) near Paris.

Oh yes, and did you notice my last name by the way ? I forgot to mention that since my grandparents on my Dad's side are from Croatia, I grew up immersed in the Slav culture.

Hopefully, you were able to follow my journey. You now understand why it's so hard for me to answer the "where do you come from?" question !
